Our Story

Born in the dim glow of a 2019 California studio, Umbra Studios didn't start with a business plan. It started with a question — what happens when artists stop creating alone?

Founded on the belief that art thrives in community.

Umbra Studios began as a collective of five artists seeking to create a space where creative expression could exist without commercial boundaries. A place where the shadows held as much beauty as the light.

Today, we represent over 120 artists across 30 countries, host exhibitions in major cities worldwide, and pioneer NFT collaborations that bridge the physical and digital art worlds. Our mission remains unchanged: to connect art lovers with extraordinary experiences that move, provoke, and inspire.

From immersive gallery installations in Tokyo to blockchain-native drops in New York, Umbra Studios is where the avant-garde finds its audience.
